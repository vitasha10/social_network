Follow the steps below to generate your APK for your application:


1. Load Android Studio (We have used Android Studio 3.0.1).

2. Open Project in folder "App Android/iSocial".


3. Open "iSocial\app\src\main\res\values\strings.xml", and change strings: "app_name" and "mgks_promo"

4. Open "iSocial\app\src\main\java\com\isocial\webview\MainActivity.java", and change "String ASWV_URL" with the URL own.

5. Change App Icon from "iSocial\app\src\main\res\raw" and "iSocial\app\src\main\res\mipmap-*" 

6. Open "iSocial\app\src\main\AndroidManifest.xml", and locate the following string:

   package="com.isocial.webview"

   and place the cursor on the word "isocial" and press SHIFT+F6 to change the package name.

7. Now you just have to generate the APK for your application.


8. Done. 


NOTE: We have used "Android Advanced WebView App" to generate our APK. You can get more information at:

https://infeeds.com/u/mgks/25019/android-smart-webview-open-source-upload. 
https://github.com/mgks/Android-SmartWebView
